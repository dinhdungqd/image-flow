Introduction

Image Flow ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome extension that helps users manage AI workflow prompts, generate images, and automate tasks on Google Labs Flow. This Privacy Policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and what controls you have.

By installing Image Flow you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the extension.

2

Data We Access & Why

The extension accesses the following categories of data solely to provide its features:

  • Active tab URL — to detect when you are on a Google Labs project page and sync the active project ID.
  • Screenshots of the visible tab — only when you explicitly trigger the crop tool (Alt+C or the crop button). The cropped image is saved to your local storage or reference image list; it is never sent to our servers.
  • Images right-clicked via the context menu — to save them to your local gallery or reference list on demand.
  • Prompt text you save to the library — stored locally in chrome.storage.local and never transmitted without your action.
  • Extension settings — stored locally (floating widget toggle, slash-command toggle, Telegram credentials you enter, etc.).

⚠️ We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, browsing history, keystrokes, form data, or any personally identifiable information beyond what you explicitly provide in the extension's settings fields.

4

Third-Party Services

The extension may communicate with the following external endpoints:

  • nguyenanhdung.io.vn/api/version — fetches the latest version number to check for updates. No personal data is sent; only a timestamped GET request is made.
  • Google OAuth2 / Google Drive — used only if you choose to enable Google Drive backup. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's own OAuth2 flow. The extension only requests the drive.file scope (access to files it creates).
  • Telegram Bot API — used only if you enter your own Telegram bot token and chat ID in settings. Notifications are sent to your personal bot; credentials are stored locally and never transmitted to our servers.
  • api.civitai.com / civitai.com — accessed only when you use the CivitAI model-search feature inside the extension UI.
  • translate.googleapis.com — used for optional prompt translation; no data is stored by us.

All third-party connections are triggered by your direct actions inside the extension. The extension does not make background requests to any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5

Local Data Storage

All user data — including prompt libraries, reference images (stored as base64), project settings, and analytics counters — is saved exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Google Drive backup feature.
  • Is cleared automatically when you uninstall the extension.
  • Can be manually deleted at any time from Chrome's extension storage settings.
6

Your Controls & Rights

  • Disable features — the floating widget, slash-command overlay, and Telegram notifications can each be turned off individually in settings.
  • Delete your data — uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data, or use Chrome DevTools to clear chrome.storage.local manually.
  • Revoke Google Drive access — visit myaccount.google.com/permissions to revoke the extension's Drive access at any time.
  • No account required — the extension works fully offline and without any account sign-in (except for the optional Google Drive feature).
7

Children's Privacy

Image Flow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this extension, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
